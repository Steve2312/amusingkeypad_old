Product introduction:

  • Customize Buttons - Bind any key to any button.
  • Hot swappable - Change switches without soldering.
  • Multimedia support - Play, Pause, Next, Prev, Vol+, Vol-, Mute, and other keycodes.
  • Simulate mouse - You can use it to do some mouse work, such as Left button, right button, wheel, and absolute coordinates positioning.
  • RGB Lighting - Customize the Full-RGB Lighting to you liking, or use the presets.
  • Low Response time - It uses a special key-switch filter algorithm to achieve the low response time.
  • Boot Loader - Built-in boot loader to keep your Keypad always up-to date.
  • Portability - Use the portable Windows software to change your configuration, and save it on your Keypad.
